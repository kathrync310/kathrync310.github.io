Starlight Fever out now

Luna Violet, also known as Hope King, is a singer and songwriter who was put on the map after coming out with her alt/pop debut album “Ultra-Violet Blue.” She grew up in Toronto, Canada and had always enjoyed alternative and classic rock, but later found her style to incorporate a pop upbeat feel. She writes all of her music herself and, with the help of her producer James Marco, makes it really shine. Since her debut album, she has come out with a few singles that have had positive feedback, but she is ready to make another hit like “Two Moons Apart”, her number one song from Ultra-Violet Blue.
